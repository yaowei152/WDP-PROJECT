How to use the Invoice Automation System


  • Step 1: Check Orders. Navigate to 'Orders Management'. Pending orders that need invoicing will have a green "Auto-Generate Invoice" button.
  • Step 2: Generate Invoice. Click the button. The AI will pre-fill the Client Name, Email, and Amount from the order history. Review the details.
  • Step 3: Confirm. Click "Confirm & Send". The invoice is created, marked as 'Sent', and the Order status updates to 'Invoiced'.
  • Step 4: Tracking. Use the 'Invoice Management' page to search for invoices by their unique code (e.g., INV-2025...).
  • Step 5: Audit. If anything goes wrong, check the 'Audit Log' to see system failures or user actions.
